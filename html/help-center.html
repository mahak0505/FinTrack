Frequently Asked

Common questions

How do I add my first expense? ▼
After logging in to your dashboard, click the "Quick Add" button at the top or use the Expenses section from the sidebar. Select the category, enter the amount, add an optional note, and hit Add. Finly will auto-suggest categories based on patterns it learns over time.
How does the AI Advisor work? ▼
The AI Advisor analyzes your spending patterns, scholarship dates, and goals to provide personalised insights. It learns from your data over time — the more you use Finly, the smarter the advice. You can also chat with it directly and ask questions like "Why am I overspending on food?" or "Help me save ₹5,000 by June."
Is Finly really free for students? ▼
Yes — Finly's core features including expense tracking, budget goals, basic AI insights, and savings planning are completely free forever for students. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core will always remain free. No credit card required at signup.
How is my financial data protected? ▼
All your data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard AES-256 and TLS 1.3. We never sell your data to third parties and we never share it with advertisers. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings.
Can I link my bank account? ▼
Currently Finly uses manual expense logging — no bank account linking is required. This is intentional: it keeps you aware and mindful of your spending. Bank integration is on our roadmap for 2026 as an optional feature for users who prefer it.
How do I set up a savings goal? ▼
Head to the Savings section from the dashboard sidebar. Click "Add Goal", enter the goal name, target amount, and target date. Finly's AI will create a week-by-week savings plan based on your scholarship cycle and current spending patterns.
I forgot my password — how do I reset it? ▼
Click "Log In" on the homepage, then click "Forgot Password" below the password field. Enter your college email address. You'll receive a reset link within 2 minutes. If you don't see it, check your spam folder or contact support.
Does Finly work on mobile? ▼
The web app is fully responsive and works great on mobile browsers. Dedicated iOS and Android apps are under development and expected to launch later in 2026. You can sign up via the web app now and your data will sync automatically to the mobile apps when they launch.
