How We Got Here

Our story

Finly didn't start in a boardroom. It started as a frustration — a spreadsheet that got too big, a bank app that showed nothing useful, and a question: "Where did all that money go?"

From that question grew a product used by thousands of students across India to take back control of their finances.

2022 — The Beginning

A spreadsheet with ambitions

What started as a personal Google Sheet to track hostel bills quickly outgrew itself. Friends started asking for copies. We knew there was something here.

2023 — First Build

Finly v1 goes live

The first version shipped to 50 beta testers. It was rough around the edges but the core — fast expense logging with smart categorisation — resonated immediately.

2024 — Growth

10,000 users & monthly analysis

We added budget tracking, monthly spending reports, and smart suggestions. Word spread. 10,000 students. Zero ads, all organic.

2025–26 — Today

AI, mobile, and beyond

AI advisor, mobile apps, and investment tracking are in progress. The mission hasn't changed — just the scale of it.
