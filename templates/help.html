How to use Finly effectively
Where is my data stored?
Since this is a client-side application, all your financial transactions, goals, and settings are currently stored locally in your browser's localStorage. Clearing your browser cache may erase this data.
How does the AI Advisor work?
To use the AI Advisor, you must input your personal free Gemini API Key in the Settings page. Finly securely packages your spending data and sends it directly from your browser to Google servers to generate context-aware financial advice without intermediate servers.
Can I change my basic profile details like Address/UK?
Yes! Navigate to the Settings page to update your Full Name, College, and Location/Address. Once saved, these details will automatically update in your sidebar and your Topbar Profile dropdown on every page, just click your initials ("AZ") at the top right!
