Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here's exactly what we collect, what we do with it, and your rights.

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create a Finly account, we collect your name, college email address, and the monthly budget you set. As you use the app, we collect expense entries you manually add including amounts, categories, and optional notes. We also collect usage analytics (pages visited, features used) to improve the product.

We do not collect your bank account details, card numbers, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data
  • To provide and improve Finly's features and AI recommendations
  • To send important account notifications and product updates
  • To generate anonymised, aggregate insights (no individual data is ever shared)
  • To respond to your support requests
3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. We do not share your data with advertisers. We may share anonymised, aggregate usage data (e.g., "students spend an average of ₹2,000 on food per month") for research purposes — but this can never be linked back to any individual user.

4. Data Security

All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3. We follow industry-standard security practices and conduct regular audits. Our infrastructure is hosted on secure, SOC-2 compliant cloud providers.

5. Your Rights
  • Access: Request a full export of your data at any time
  • Correction: Update or correct any information in your account
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all data permanently from Settings
  • Portability: Export your expense history as a CSV at any time
6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@finly.in
